The locals best!

Bizzarro's Italian

Enjoy the comfort and peice of the local italian restaurant Bizzaro. Sure to cure your appetite for genuine italian food.

Witham and Blues

Witham and Blues is a traditionally american style diner. Outside you will see the american NCPD police car and also a New york cab! Enjoy a family meal at this 5* rated restaurant.

The Ball House

The Ball House is a quiet home style restaurant serving up traditional dishes to perfection. Situated on the outskirts of Boston on the A16 this is a must for any visitor.

The resteraunts above are all local to Boston or in a very close proximity. Boston offers a host of different style resteraunts including Turkish, Chinese, Indian, Romanian, Italian and more. Over the years many people have chosen to stop in Boston and cook great food, there are a multitude to choose from and some of the best are listed here so be sure to check them out if you are passing by.

The top 6 shown on the right have been selected from trip advisor in order of favourite, this way you can decide which you may want to visit and what you like the best.
